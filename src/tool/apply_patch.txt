Use apply_patch with:

- *** Begin Patch … *** End Patch
- One or more ops, each starting with exactly one header:
  - *** Add File: <path> then only + lines
  - *** Delete File: <path>
  - *** Update File: <path> optionally followed by *** Move to: <path>
  Edits are file-oriented diffs. Headers are mandatory.
