Launch a subagent for complex or broad tasks.

Use when:
- the task is multi-step or open-ended
- broad codebase exploration is needed
- a specialized agent fits better than direct tool use
- a slash command must be executed through a subagent

Do not use when a direct read, glob, or grep is faster.

When invoking:
- choose the right subagent type
- state whether it should research or modify
- specify the expected final output clearly
- resume with task_id when continuing prior work
